Ashley Silcox

Reading has various uses and outlets. One of its most prevalent applications is that it allows for further growth, development, and overall successin school and life. But it also opens the door for adventure and expanding of one's understanding of the world, taking journeys to faraway places, exposure to new ideas, insight into oneself. Finding yourself in the story you are reading can bring comfort, freedom, validation, and even new friendships. Literature can also be a window into the world of someone else; opening the door to new experiences, thoughts, ideals, and perspective. A child who can access literature gains the key to discovery.
