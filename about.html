Books are a Gateway to Imagination

For eight years I was a teacher and administrator in elementary education, working with students in grades K-6. I completed a Master's degree in Reading and Literacy at the University of Utah in December 2019, and am currently working as a doctoral student in Reading and Literacy at the Univesity of Utah. For the past six years, I have privately tutored students who struggle with reading, and have seen the wonder of books come into their lives as they develop and enhance the skills they need to be successful readers. Literacy at home is crucial for your child's reading development. So, what can you do with your child at home to help support their reading development and progress?

  • Read with your child! Allow them to ask questions, and ask questions as you are reading.
  • As your child plays, interact with them and incorporate literary elements!
  • Take them to the library.
  • Let them see you read! Engage in reading simply by each read your own book!
